The Realms of ArcanaGate

  • Ebonreach: A shadowy swamp filled with cursed whispers and lost souls.
  • Luminaris: A floating city suspended by ancient arcane crystals, glowing with blue light.
  • Thornwood: A twisted, sentient forest that traps intruders in illusions and thorns.
  • Skyridge: Towering mountains once ruled by dragons; echoes of roars still shake the cliffs.
  • Veilmarsh: A fog-choked marsh where time behaves unpredictably.
  • Obsidian Hollow: A crater of black stone — origin unknown — pulsing with dark energy.
